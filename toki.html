TOKI TOKI is an environmental design project on conceptualizing a restaurant. TOKI TOKI would contain a specialized changing menu of beverages, meals, and desserts that are inspired by Korean cuisine. It would attract remote workers, students, and creatives of the twin cities looking for a homey yet modern space to work for hours in. The branding behind TOKI TOKI centers around the image of a rabbit, a distinct black, blue, and red color palette, and wood-like print illustrations.
